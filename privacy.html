Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-22

This Privacy Policy explains how this camera application ("the App") handles information and user data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

The App does not require user accounts and does not directly collect personal information such as name, email address, or phone number.

1.2 Camera, Photos & EXIF Data

The App accesses the device camera and media storage to:

  • Capture photos
  • Save images locally on your device

Photos may contain EXIF metadata, including location (GPS) data, if enabled by the system camera settings.

  • EXIF and GPS data are stored only on your device
  • The App does not transmit photos or EXIF data to any server

1.3 Location Data

Location data may be embedded into photo EXIF metadata only if:

  • Location services are enabled on the device
  • The system camera framework provides GPS metadata

The App does not use location data for tracking and does not upload or process location data remotely.

2. Firebase Services

2.1 Firebase Analytics

The App uses Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data such as:

  • App interactions
  • Feature usage
  • Device and operating system information

This data is aggregated and anonymized and is used solely to improve app functionality and user experience. No photos, EXIF data, or personal content are collected.

2.2 Firebase Crashlytics

Firebase Crashlytics is used to detect and diagnose application crashes. Collected information may include:

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • App state at the time of a crash

This information is used only for debugging and stability improvements.

3. In-App Purchases

The App offers optional In-App Purchases processed through Google Play Billing.

  • Payment information is handled entirely by Google
  • The App does not collect or store payment details
  • Purchase status may be stored locally to unlock features

4. How We Use Information

Information collected is used solely to:

  • Operate core camera functionality
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Diagnose crashes
  • Enable purchased features

We do not sell user data, share data for advertising, or perform user profiling.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Photos, and EXIF data are stored locally on your device
  • Firebase data is transmitted securely using encrypted connections
  • No user-generated content is stored on our servers

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase Analytics
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics
  • Google Play Billing

7. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since no user accounts are required, the App is suitable for general audiences.

8. User Control

You can:

  • Disable location tagging in system camera settings
  • Revoke camera, storage, or analytics permissions via Android system settings
  • Delete photos from your device at any time
  • Uninstall the App to remove all local data

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
